Speed Sail

A performance app for Performance Dinghys

Any dinghy sailor knows that it’s a constant battle to keep performing at your max. Unfortunately, we can't tell how flat the boat really is, are you too far forward to back, what is our speed and how much did we lose in that tack? The SpeedSail app aims to break the tradition of guesswork and feeling by giving you real time information on:

  • Speed
  • Heading
  • Weather
  • Balance
  • Trim
  • GPS Location
Improve your performance with the power of your smartphone, simply attach to your deck and configure the perfect balance.

Not on the water?

The race management module allows you to easily calculate your handicap race results on the fly. Add the contenders as and when you like (even mid race), Start and finish boats with a single press. Support DNF and lap differences for shortened races.

Using Speed Sail

Gauges

Swipe left or Right on the screen to switch though the Gauges Available.
Alternatively you can use to access the side menu and select a Gauge.
Pressing any of the s will skip you around.

Real Time

The real time gauge is the Everyman screen. It shows heading at the top or on the compass dial, speed is textually in the centre of the screen and shown in scale by the coloured ring. Weather data is at the bottom of the screen with current forecast on the left, next forecast on the right and the time difference in the centre in hours and minutes. Wind direction is shown by the yellow arrow on the compass dial. Balance is the red dot moving around in the centre of the dial.

Balance

Balance is shown using a red dot over a grid a red path is generated as your balance changes. This can be cleared using "Clear Trail" button.

Navigation

With the support of Google maps you can see where you are in the world. Standard Google maps controls apply plus you can clear your path with the "Clear Trail" button.

Speed

Your all in one speedometer. Speed is shown textually in the centre of the screen, with a colour changing gauge surrounding it. A graph has been provided to track changes in your speed.

Controls

Controls can be accessed from any Gauge by pressing the "Controls" Text at the bottom of the screen.

Recording

To start recording press the by the controls text.
To stop recording press the red by the controls text. You will then be prompted to save your recording by a notification in the top right.

Playback

To select a previous recording simply select from the drop down box. The "Current" options simply shows current data.
Use the slider to navigate your recording.
Use the & to play and pause the replay.

Race Management

Built to help OD's run Handicap racing without all that maths.

Add A boat

Pressing "Add Boat" button brings up the form to add a boat. Once done press "Add" to add a boat to the race. The form can be hidden by pressing the "Add Boat" button again.

Running a race

If you don't know how to manually run a race please look it up here
To start a race simply press the "Start Race" button, the start time is then calculated. If for some reason you need to restart the race you can use the "Reset" button.
The overall amount of laps to be raced is entered into the "Laps" box
Boats are finished individually by pressing "Finish" against the boat. If you make a mistake you can unfinished a boat with "Not Finished".
LD stands for lap difference, to be used if a boat has done more or less than the standard laps.
To DNF or remove a boat the options can be found under the button.

Settings

Settings can be accessed though the button. Use this to configure the app to give data the way you want it.
You can also delete recordings from this screen.
